Voltorb

Net profit & loss for the high-stakes professional poker cohort — live freezeout tournaments, $11,000+ buy-ins. Data spans 2012–present; default view shows 2020 onwards (change it above the leaderboard).

Methodology. Net P&L = winnings − every buy-in (including events a player entered and busted). That requires a complete entrant list, so a tournament feeds the leaderboard only when its full field is published. Events without a full field are listed in Coverage as excluded with a reason — they never affect the numbers. Backing/staking is ignored; figures are gross of any deals. Re-entry counts default to 1 per player unless recorded. All amounts in USD.
