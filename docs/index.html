High-stakes poker · net profit & loss

Voltorb

Who in the high-stakes professional cohort is actually winning or losing — live freezeout tournaments, $7,000+ buy-ins, with every buy-in counted (busts included). Data spans 2012–present; the leaderboard defaults to 2020 onward.

Net P&L leaderboard

# Player Events ITM Buy-ins Winnings Net P&L ROI
Methodology. Net P&L = winnings − every buy-in (including events a player entered and busted). That requires a complete entrant list, so a tournament feeds the leaderboard only when its full field is published — which in practice means freezeouts. Events without a full field are listed in Coverage as excluded with a reason; they never affect the numbers. Backing/staking is ignored; figures are gross of any deals. Re-entry counts default to 1 per player unless recorded. All amounts in USD.
